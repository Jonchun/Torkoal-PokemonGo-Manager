Torkoal.com was founded both for me to learn more about Reverse Engineering, Python, APIs, and new technologies and for me to fill a gap that the game Pokémon GO doesn't have. I started a related projects and any public ones will be released on Torkoal.

That being said, I need to state the standard disclaimer:

In it's current state, Pokémon Go does NOT have an official API. Using any of the projects related to the unofficial/internal API is technically grounds for a ban. That being said, I believe that if a company bans people for using most of the projects here, the game probably isn't worth supporting anyways. Torkoal.com is not responsible for any bans or suspensions of your account.

IMPORTANT
Currently, Pokémon Go does NOT support token-based authentication by a third party. This means that you have to send your password to us (encrypted over SSL) and that Torkoal does have access to a decrypted, plain-text version of your login credentials. While we would NEVER store your credentials, we also take no responsibility in the event that you are compromised. We make our best efforts to protect your data and securely handle it, but Torkoal.com comes with no guarantees of any kind whatsoever.
